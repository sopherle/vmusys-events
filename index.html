VMUSYS Monthly Coffee Break

Join the open coffee break hosted by the VMUSYS board. An opportunity for scientific staff to meet, exchange informally, and strengthen the network within the department. We meet every last Tuesday of the month, at Zwei Grad Bistro on the Green Floor (CHN). The coffee will be paid for by the VMUSYS board.
